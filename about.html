What does an interior designer do?
Interior designers plan and create functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces by considering factors like layout, color schemes, furniture, and decor.
Why should I hire an interior designer?
Hiring an interior designer can bring expertise, creativity, and a professional touch to your project.
How much does it cost to hire an interior designer?
The cost varies based on factors like the designer's experience, the scope of the project, and location.

What does a contractor do?
Contractors oversee the construction or renovation of a project. They manage subcontractors, ensure work meets codes and regulations, and often handle permits and logistics.
How do I choose the right contractor for my project?
Look for experienced and licensed contractors, check references, and review their portfolio.
How can I estimate the cost of my project?
The cost depends on factors like the size of the project, materials used, and labor. Contractors can provide detailed estimates based on these factors.
What permits do I need, and who obtains them?
Depending on your location and the scope of the project, various permits may be required.
